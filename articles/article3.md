# Security Operations Center (SOC): Tools, Techniques, and Incident Response

**Date:** April 2026  
**Category:** SOC  

---

## Introduction
A Security Operations Center (SOC) is a centralized unit that monitors, detects, and responds to cyber threats in real time.

---

## What is SOC?
SOC is a team of security professionals responsible for protecting systems and networks using tools and processes.

---

## Key Functions
- Continuous monitoring  
- Threat detection  
- Incident response  
- Log analysis  

---

## SOC Tools
- SIEM  
- IDS/IPS  
- EDR  
- Firewalls  
- Threat Intelligence  

---

## Incident Response Process
1. Preparation  
2. Detection  
3. Analysis  
4. Containment  
5. Eradication  
6. Recovery  

---

## Real-World Example
Unauthorized login attempts can be detected and blocked by SOC tools.

---

## Importance
SOC helps detect threats early and prevent cyber attacks.

---

## Challenges
- Alert fatigue  
- Large data volume  
- Evolving threats  

---

## Future
AI and automation will improve SOC efficiency.

---

## Conclusion
SOC is essential for modern cybersecurity and protects organizations from threats.
